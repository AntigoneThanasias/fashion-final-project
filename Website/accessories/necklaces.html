Necklaces

Lisby Loriis jewerly focuses on earth elements.

Designer Laureta Ismailaj is fascinated by rock crystals, as they are rough and bold in texture but soft in their shine.

Having this duality in character reminds Ismailaj of the her muse for fashion. The idea that fashion is feminine, but with an edge to it.
