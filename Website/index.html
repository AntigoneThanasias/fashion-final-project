Welcome to the Designs of Laureta Ismailaj

Here you can satisfy your cravings for fashion and art.

"Generating my color story is very important. My inspiration for colors comes from nature, from everyday life, from pictures that I capture. Like when the rain falls over those colors of flowers, trees, and leaves on the ground. I try to pick objects that I feel connection towards. Not only by colors but the stories they tell as well. Colors that I focus on are monochromatic, complimentary and then I attempt to cohesively use them throughout my line. LisbyLoriisfunctions through my fascination of architectural shapes of the old classical buildings, Renaissance art works, or even sometimes a small jewel embellishment from different paintings."

-Laureta Ismailaj
